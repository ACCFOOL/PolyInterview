AI Research Assistant

Currently enrolled in or recently completed a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, or related field.
Strong programming skills in Python with experience in ML frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX).
Solid understanding of machine learning fundamentals, deep learning, and statistical methods.
Ability to read and implement ideas from academic papers.

Experience with large language models (LLMs), transformers, or generative AI.
Familiarity with NLP, computer vision, or reinforcement learning.
Prior research experience or publications in ML/AI conferences.
Experience with high-performance computing or GPU clusters.
Knowledge of experiment tracking tools (Weights & Biases, MLflow).

Maintain clean, reproducible code and documentation.
