# Company Name
**Meta**

# Job Position
**Software Engineer**

# Job Description
Minimum Qualifications:
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, relevant technical field, or equivalent practical experience.
2+ years of programming experience in one or more of the following: C/C++, Java, Python, Go, or Rust.
Experience building and shipping production software.

Preferred Qualifications:
Experience with distributed systems, large-scale systems design, or infrastructure.
Familiarity with React, React Native, or mobile development.
Experience with machine learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow).
Contributions to open source projects.

Responsibilities:
Develop and maintain software solutions that serve billions of users worldwide.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams including product managers, designers, and data scientists.
Participate in code reviews and contribute to engineering best practices.
Debug production issues and implement solutions with minimal guidance.
Mentor junior engineers and contribute to team growth.

Skills:
Algorithms and data structures
System design
Code quality and testing
Cross-functional collaboration
